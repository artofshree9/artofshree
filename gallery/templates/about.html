" A small story about an artist"

From watching creativity at home to expressing my own story through every stroke.

My journey with art started at a very young age. I always had a natural interest in creativity, but the real inspiration came from my aunt, who was incredibly talented in painting, stitching, and many other creative skills. Watching her create beautiful things sparked something inside me. I began learning everything at home, exploring art in my own way. My aunt always encouraged me to paint and express myself, which helped me build confidence in my abilities. Taking that confidence forward, I participated in my first painting competition and secured third position. That moment became a turning point for me. It motivated me to keep going and believe in my talent. After that, I continued participating in many competitions, growing with each experience and improving my skills along the way. For me, art is not just a hobby — it is a part of who I am. It reflects my journey, my growth, and my passion for creating something meaningful.
